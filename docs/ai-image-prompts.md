# St. John Biocare — AI hero image prompts (New Patients & About)

**How to use:** paste each `PROMPT` block into **ChatGPT → Create image** (GPT-image /
GPT-4o). Choose a **wide / landscape** result. Download it and send it back to me —
I'll optimize it to WebP, place it, and wire it into the page.

**Why they're built this way:** these are **full-bleed** heroes (a warm photo spans
the whole hero with the headline set over a soft green scrim on the left), so each
image is **wordless**, **landscape**, keeps the **subject on the right two-thirds**,
and leaves the **left third soft/uncluttered** for the headline. This is deliberately
different from renalcarematters' circular-vignette look.

## 1 · New Patients — hero

```
FILE NAME: new-patients-hero.png
IMAGE TYPE: Full-bleed landscape hero (wordless editorial photo)
ASPECT RATIO: 3:2 landscape (wide)

PROMPT:
A warm, photorealistic editorial photograph, wide 3:2 landscape orientation, of a
kind Filipina dialysis nurse in soft forest-green scrubs gently welcoming a new
patient and their adult daughter at the intake of a bright, modern Philippine
dialysis center. The nurse is turned toward them with a reassuring, unforced smile,
one hand offering a clipboard; the middle-aged male patient looks relieved and
hopeful; the daughter stands supportively beside him. Warm natural morning light
through large windows, clean cream-and-sage interior, gentle shallow depth of field.

Composition: place the people in the RIGHT two-thirds of the frame; keep the LEFT
third calm and uncluttered — a softly blurred cream wall and warm light — as open
negative space for a headline. Documentary-realistic, not staged or stocky.

Colour & mood: warm, reassuring, hopeful; a palette harmonizing with forest green
#006028 and life green #089838 over cream #FBF7F0 and warm sand #F3E9D8, with at
most a single small touch of heart-crimson #E02048; airy and bright, never
clinical-cold or dark. Filipino people and setting, Metro Manila.

Absolutely NO text of any kind — no title, caption, label, logo, or watermark.

NEGATIVE INSTRUCTIONS:
Avoid: any text/letters/logos/watermarks; dark, dim, or charcoal backgrounds;
cold clinical blue tones; cluttered busy background; more than three people;
distorted hands or faces; extra fingers; cartoon or illustration style; heavy
HDR, neon, or oversaturation; stocky staged cheesiness; the subject centered or
filling the left third (keep the left open for text).

QUALITY CHECK:
Wide landscape. People occupy the right two-thirds; the left third is soft, open
negative space for a headline. Warm morning light, brand greens over cream, one
optional small crimson accent. Filipino nurse + patient + caregiver, hopeful and
natural. Correct hands and faces. No text, no logo, no watermark anywhere.
```
