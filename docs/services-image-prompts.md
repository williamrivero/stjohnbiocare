# St. John Biocare — Image Shoot Brief

**For the GPT Image Generator (GPT-image / GPT-4o).** Written as an art-direction
brief: each image says what it must *make the viewer feel*, then gives a clean,
copy-paste prompt. Every image shares one house style so the finished site reads
like a single, intentional photo shoot — never stock scraps.

---

## The house style (the shared DNA of every image)

> Warm, hopeful, editorial-quality photograph. A real Filipino dialysis-care
> setting. Natural soft **morning light**, a palette of creams and warm neutrals,
> with **emerald / forest-green scrubs** as the one signature color that recurs
> across every frame. Shot on a full-frame camera, 35mm lens, shallow depth of
> field. Photorealistic, premium, and dignified — a place of excellence and
> belonging, never sterile, crowded, clinical-cold, or frightening.

**Always avoid:** any text, watermarks, or logos in the image · visible blood or
needles · harsh fluorescent lighting · stocky, over-lit corporate posing ·
exaggerated grins · cluttered backgrounds · distorted hands or faces.

## Generation settings

- **Aspect / size:** choose **Landscape · 1536 × 1024 (3:2)** for every image
  below. Do not use square.
- **Variations:** generate **2–3 per prompt**, then keep the most natural one.
- **Headline safety (heroes only):** the people sit on the **right**; the
  **left third stays calm** so website text can rest there without fighting the
  photo.

## 1 · Home — Hero

**Carries the headline:** *"Excellent dialysis, for every family — not just a
few."* The frame must show **excellence** *and* **family** — a patient held by
both a nurse and a loved one, with the quiet suggestion that many families are
cared for here, to the same standard.

```
A warm, hopeful, editorial-quality photograph for the homepage hero of a Filipino
dialysis-care foundation. The scene shows excellent dialysis care embracing a whole
family: an older Filipino patient resting comfortably and with dignity in a modern
dialysis treatment chair, a caring Filipino nurse in an emerald-green scrub suit
attending attentively on one side, and a loving adult family member (a son or
daughter) seated close on the other side, holding the patient's hand and sharing a
hopeful smile. Real human connection — the feeling that no one goes through this
alone. Behind them, softly out of focus, a bright, clean, well-run dialysis floor
with a few more chairs and nurses, suggesting many families are cared for here to
the same high standard. Composition: wide 3:2 landscape, people weighted to the
right two-thirds, the left third calmer (soft daylight, gentle blur) for headline
text. Full-frame camera, 35mm lens, shallow depth of field, natural soft morning
light through a window. Warm creams and neutrals, emerald-green scrubs as the
signature accent. Photorealistic, dignified, hopeful. No text, logos, blood, or
needles.
```
